What we sing

This year, the mission of Joyful Noise has been to present biblical truths through songs that are relatable to and enjoyed by the average college student. Christianity is a complex thing and can be confusing at times so, we've tried to find aspects of it that arise in our culture's music and present these aspects through our Christian worldview. Beyond wanting to convey that we're Christian and hold Scripture to be true, what we want most is to let people know that they are loved unconditionally.
